KFC

KFC was founded by “Colonel” Harland Sanders in Kentucky in 1930 during the Great Depression. The first Kentucky Fried Chicken franchise was opened in Salt Lake City, Utah in 1952 by Pete Harman, who introduced the “Bucket O'Chicken” in 1957.
